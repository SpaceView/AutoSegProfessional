when the annotation draw is shifted or rotated, manually edit points must be manually upgraded
标注的旋转和平移过程中，手动修改的点必须手动同步更新

NOTE：the pan, zoom, and rotation operation can only be applied to geometric annotation draw, NOT finished annotation.
        these functions need assistant from the <TEMPLATE> tab.
        use a template which is useless in annotation.
注意：旋转平移缩放操作只能是标注时和几何图形，已经完成的标注不能操作。
         这些操作需要<TEMPLATE>页面的辅助。

Press KEYBOARD left, right, up, down key  to pan
按键盘上的下上下左右键可以平衡标注线

press the rotate button (on the left panel) to rotate
按左侧的旋转按钮可旋转

press the scaling button to scale (on the right <TEMPLATE> tab to zoom, please note there is a scaling factor
按右侧<TEMPLATE>页面下的“SCALE”键可以绽放，注意设置缩放因子。

这里可以设置旋转的角度
